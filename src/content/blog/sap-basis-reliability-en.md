SAP Basis reliability is not a single task. It is the result of many small operational decisions made consistently over time.

For me, reliability starts with discipline:

- clear monitoring priorities
- predictable change handling
- backup and recovery readiness
- documentation that can still be used under pressure

A stable SAP landscape is not only about preventing outages. It is also about reducing uncertainty for the business. When systems are understandable, observable and recoverable, the organization can move faster with less fear.

That is the direction of this site as well: practical thinking, calm execution and long-term technical trust.
